Need help getting started?
Here's a few ideas for your letter!

  • Write a letter to yourself a year from now

  • Write something to your childhood self, or yourself far in the future

  • A letter to an old friend of yours, who you haven't seen in a long time

  • Something for a family member, or even a pet

  • A letter to yourself during a certain time, like a difficult moment, or a really happy one!

  • Write a letter to a stranger, or someone you don't know very well, you could introduce yourself, tell them a story, or give them a nice inspirational message!

  • Write something to someone who you admire

  • A letter towards your favourite characters

  • Something to address your unspoken thoughts, the things you wish you could say

  • A letter to the world, or even the universe

  • Write a letter to your future children, or grandchildren

  • Write to someone who you never got to say goodbye to

When you're done writing your letter, check one of the boxes below to choose whether you'd like to keep your letter private, or make it public. If you'd like to make it public, other users will be able to read it on the 'Read Letters' page! After that, click on the 'Finish Letter' button on the bottom right of your screen, sign in, and you're done!
